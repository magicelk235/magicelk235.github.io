Why Spyglass needs Google access

Rendered previews need to read the document from your Google Drive. That is the only reason Spyglass asks to sign in. Here is exactly what it does and does not do.

  • Read-only

    Spyglass uses the drive.readonly scope. It cannot edit, delete, or share your files. It can only read them to draw the preview.

  • Used only to render previews

    Access is used for one thing: fetching the document export so Spyglass can show the first page. Nothing else.

  • Stays on your Mac

    Previews are cached locally on your machine. There is no Spyglass server. Your documents are never uploaded to us or to any third party.

  • Email only to show the account

    Your email is used only to display which Google account is signed in. It is not sent anywhere.

  • Reversible

    Sign out at any time. Doing so clears the stored tokens and revokes the grant at Google.

Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. Spyglass requests read-only access with the drive.readonly scope, so it can never edit, delete, or share anything. Previews are cached only on your Mac. There is no server and your documents are never uploaded to us or any third party. See the full Privacy Policy for details.

What do I get for free?

The free tier shows a branded info card for all six stub types. You get the document icon, title, owner, and a link to open it on Google. It works instantly, offline, with no sign-in. It is a complete app, not a trial.

How do I unlock the paid features?

Buy Spyglass on Gumroad for $9. You get a license key by email. Paste the key into the Spyglass menu-bar app and sign in with Google, and rendered previews turn on. One payment, no subscription.

Which file types work?

All six Google Drive stub types: .gdoc, .gsheet, .gslides, .gdraw, .gform, and .gsite. Rendered previews cover Docs, Sheets, Slides, and Drawings. Forms and Sites cannot be exported by Google, so they always show the info card.

Does it work offline?

The free info card works fully offline. Rendered previews need a connection to fetch the document from Google Drive. If you are offline or the network is slow, Spyglass falls back to the info card, so a preview never hangs or comes up blank.
